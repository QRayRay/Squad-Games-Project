About GCG

The GCG Club was created by Raymond Quintana on December 1st, 2019. This club started out as a phrase and turned into a life style for Raymond. Raymonds days would consist of weight training (Gains), food (Raising Canes Chicken), and then play video games (Games) at the end of each day / night. Raymonds friends would soon join him in this lifestyle over the years and expand on the clubs core values.

We now compete in yearly fitness compentitions accross the state of Texas. We also hold Club competions against other club members for cash prizes. Below are the 3 core values this club holds. Each value will have a list of events, food and activities that we are involved in daily.

Gains

  • Weight Lifting
  • Running
  • Strength and Conditioning
  • Power Lifting
  • Rock Climbing

Canes

  • Ricky's Hot Chicken Sandwhich
  • Pop Eye's Chicken Sandwhich

  • Layne's Chicken Sandwhich
